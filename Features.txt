# 🚜 Smart Farm Machinery Booking Platform

## Final Feature List 

---

# 1️⃣ User Registration & Login

The system supports **two types of users**:

### Farmers

Users who want to rent agricultural machinery.

### Equipment Owners

Users who own tractors or farm equipment and rent them out.

### Registration Details (Demo Prototype)

Users register with:

* Name
* Email address
* Password
* Aadhaar number *(demo identity reference only)*
* User role *(Farmer / Owner)*

⚠️ Aadhaar number is used **only for demonstration purposes** and is **not verified with government APIs**.

### Login

Users log in using:

* Email
* Password

---

# 2️⃣ Equipment Listing (Owner Side)

Equipment owners can list their machinery.

Details include:

* Equipment name
* Equipment type
* Equipment images
* **Price per hour**
* Driver availability
* Driver hourly charge *(optional)*
* Equipment location
* Availability schedule

---

# 3️⃣ Supported Equipment Types

Examples of supported machines:

* Tractor
* Rotavator
* Harvester
* Seed drill
* Sprayer
* Plough
* Water tanker

---

# 4️⃣ Equipment Search & Filtering

Farmers can search equipment based on:

* Equipment type
* Location
* Price
* Availability

This allows farmers to **quickly find nearby machinery**.

---

# 5️⃣ Map-Based Equipment Discovery

The system uses **OpenStreetMap demo integration**.

Features include:

* Display nearby machinery on map
* Show equipment location
* Show distance from farmer

---

# 6️⃣ Real-Time Availability Calendar

Each machine has a **booking calendar**.

Farmers can see:

* Available time slots
* Booked time slots

This prevents **double bookings**.

---

# 7️⃣ Hourly Pricing System

All rentals use **hourly pricing only**.

Example:

* Tractor → ₹500/hour
* Harvester → ₹900/hour

Farmers select:

* Date
* Number of hours

---

# 8️⃣ With Driver / Without Driver Option

Farmers can choose whether to rent machinery:

### With Driver

Charges include:

* Equipment hourly charge
* Driver hourly charge

Example:

| Item    | Cost      |
| ------- | --------- |
| Tractor | ₹500/hour |
| Driver  | ₹200/hour |
| Total   | ₹700/hour |

---

### Without Driver

Farmer provides their own operator.

Charges include:

* Equipment hourly charge only.

Example:

Tractor → ₹500/hour

---

# 9️⃣ Owner Approval Booking System

Bookings require **owner approval**.

### Booking Flow

1. Farmer selects equipment.
2. Chooses **with driver or without driver**.
3. Sends booking request.
4. Owner reviews request.
5. Owner **accepts or rejects** booking.

---

# 🔟 Notification System

Notifications are sent for key updates.

### Farmer Notifications

* Booking accepted/rejected
* Equipment arrival updates
* Payment reminders

### Owner Notifications

* New booking requests
* Booking confirmations
* Payment updates

---

Vehicle tracking uses **OpenStreetMap**.

### Farmer Side

Farmers can see:

* Vehicle location

* Estimated arrival time (ETA)

### Owner Side

Owners can track:

* Vehicle movement
* Route history
* Work progress

---

# 1️⃣2️⃣ Ratings & Reviews

Farmers can rate equipment owners based on:

* Equipment condition
* Service quality
* Timeliness

This builds **trust in the platform**.

---

# 1️⃣3️⃣ Emergency Equipment Request

Farmers can send **urgent machinery requests**.

Nearby owners receive notifications.

---

# 1️⃣4️⃣ AI-Based Equipment Recommendation

The system suggests suitable machines based on **farming activities**.

Example:

* Land preparation → Rotavator
* Harvesting → Harvester

---

# 1️⃣5️⃣ Seasonal Demand Prediction

The system predicts **equipment demand based on farming seasons**.

Example:

* Harvest season → Higher harvester demand
* Ploughing season → Higher tractor demand

---

# 1️⃣6️⃣ Weather Forecast Integration

The app shows **local weather forecasts** to help farmers plan agricultural work.

---

# 1️⃣7️⃣ Guided Voice Booking Feature 🎤

The app includes a **Simple Guided Voice Booking Assistant**.

Instead of full AI conversation, the app asks **step-by-step questions** and processes voice responses.

### Voice Interaction Flow

1. User presses **voice booking button 🎤**
2. App asks guided questions
3. User answers using voice
4. App extracts keywords
5. Booking details are generated

Example:

App: *Which machine do you want?*
User: Tractor

App: *For how many hours?*
User: 3 hours

App: *Do you need a driver?*
User: Yes

App: *Confirm booking tractor for 3 hours with driver?*

---

### Technologies Used

Voice recognition:

* Flutter package **speech_to_text**

Voice guidance:

* Flutter package **flutter_tts**

These packages enable:

* Voice input (Speech → Text)
* Voice prompts (Text → Speech)

---

### Conversation State Manager

The system tracks booking progress using **conversation steps**:

Step 0 → Machine selection
Step 1 → Duration
Step 2 → Driver option
Step 3 → Confirmation

---

### Supported Languages

Voice recognition can support:

* English (`en_IN`)
* Tamil (`ta_IN`)
* Hindi (`hi_IN`)

Example Tamil command:

“எனக்கு டிராக்டர் வேண்டும்”

---

### Safety Confirmation Screen

Because voice recognition may make mistakes, the system always shows a **confirmation screen**.

Example:

Machine: Tractor
Duration: 3 Hours
Driver: Yes

[Confirm Booking]

---

# 1️⃣8️⃣ Payment Integration

Payments are implemented using **Stripe sandbox testing environment**.

Supported payment methods:

* Card payments
* Online payment simulation in test mode

---

# 1️⃣9️⃣ Payment Methods

Two payment options are available.

### Pre-Payment

Farmer pays **100% during booking**.

### Partial Payment

Payment split into:

* **25% advance payment**
* **75% after work completion**

---

# 2️⃣0️⃣ Insurance Partner Integration

To protect expensive machinery, the platform may partner with:

* ICICI Lombard
* Bajaj Allianz General Insurance
* HDFC ERGO General Insurance

These services provide **equipment damage coverage**.

---

# 2️⃣1️⃣ Damage & Insurance Claim System

If machinery is damaged during usage:

### Step 1

Owner submits **damage report with photos**.

### Step 2

System evaluates damage.

### Step 3

Two outcomes:

**Minor Damage**

* Farmer pays repair cost.

**Major Damage**

* Insurance claim is initiated through partner company.

### Step 4

Insurance provider processes claim.
